Privacy Policy

How Mekmaa handles your information.

Mekmaa collects information you submit through forms such as account registration, contact enquiries and booking requests. This information is used only to respond to your request, manage access or support facility operations.

Personal information may include your name, email address, phone number and booking-related details. Mekmaa does not use this information for unrelated purposes without a clear operational reason.

If you need to correct or update the information you submitted, contact the Mekmaa team directly through the contact page.
