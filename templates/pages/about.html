Our Story

Created to make quality sports facilities more accessible.

Mekmaa was established with a clear purpose: to provide a dependable indoor environment where people can play sports regardless of weather, age or experience level.

We recognised that many players needed more than an empty court. They needed proper facilities, flexible booking options, professional coaching and a team that genuinely cared about their progress.

Today, Mekmaa brings all of those elements together under one roof. Our facility supports cricket, futsal, badminton and table tennis, alongside structured coaching sessions designed mainly for children and teenagers.
