Booking & Refund Policy

Booking changes and refunds.

Booking requests are subject to review, scheduling availability and confirmation by Mekmaa where applicable.

If a session needs to be changed, players should contact the team as early as possible. Rescheduling options depend on slot availability and the nature of the request.

Refund handling, if applicable, should be discussed directly with Mekmaa so the team can review the booking situation and provide the most accurate next step.
